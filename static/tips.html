Tips and Tricks

  • First off remember that this weekend is all about learning and having fun! So stay relaxed and have fun.
  • If you run into trouble don't panic and shut down. Talk it out amongst your team and brainstorm. Work off of each other and you might come up with a solution.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from other people. You're not gonna know everything, it's not bad to ask questions.
  • Although it is a hackathon you may need a break every once and a while. You'll think better with a clearer head.
  • We really want you to appreciate the things that you've learned and the things that you can use. So remember to use good coding practice and keep your code as simple as you can. Always remember KISS (Keep It Simple Stupid)
  • Some good ideals to keep in mind while your coding.
  • GitHub and Stack Overflow are awesome tools and can save you in countless ways. Use them wisely!
