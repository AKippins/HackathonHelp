Countdown to Judging!!!

Objective

In celebration of Namco Bandai's recently expired patent, we've decided to have the 7 Hour Hackathon in their honor. For this Hackathon you will be tasked with making a loading screen game. The loading screen game changed the way we think about loading screens and didn't leave us with boredom while waiting.


Guidelines

  • You need some element so that we know that the game is loading...
  • Try to make the game interactive and fun.
  • There's no requirement on language for this one, as long as the game is dope.
  • Be creative, there's no bounds on what you can and can't do!
  • Remember normal loading screens are boring. Whatever your creative minds can come up with will be better!
